Current Activities

Graduation

The graduation ceremony celebrates the achievements of
students reaching an academic milestone. The event features
speeches, the awarding of diplomas, and moments of
reflection and joy. Graduates, dressed in caps and gowns, are
honored for their hard work and are encouraged to embrace
their future opportunities.

VDEG’s 9th Volunteer Camp

VDEG’s 9th Volunteer Camp brings together dedicated
volunteers for a dynamic and inspiring experience.
Participants engage in collaborative activities, community
service projects, and team-building exercises. The camp
fosters a spirit of camaraderie and personal growth while
making a positive impact on the community. It’s a
celebration of commitment, teamwork, and the joy of giving back.

International Volunteer Day


International Volunteer Day honors the dedication and
impact of volunteers worldwide. Celebrated through events
and activities, it highlights the essential role of volunteers in
communities, showcasing their efforts and inspiring others to
get involved. The day is a tribute to their selfless
contributions and the positive changes they bring to the
world.

Ingoude Company Volunteer Camp

The Ingoude Company Volunteer Camp is a vibrant gathering
where members come together to engage in meaningful
community service. The camp features collaborative projects,
skill-building activities, and team bonding experiences. It’s an
opportunity for participants to contribute to social causes
while fostering a sense of teamwork and corporate
responsibility.

Open UP 2024

Open UP 2024 is an engaging event designed for those
interested in becoming group members. The activity features
interactive sessions, networking opportunities, and an
introduction to the group's values and goals. It provides a
welcoming environment for new members to connect, learn,
and explore how they can contribute to and grow with the
group.
